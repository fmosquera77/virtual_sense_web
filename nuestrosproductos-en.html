OUR PRODUCTS

Heart Failure

Our digital platform for heart failure includes a patient app for daily condition tracking and a healthcare team dashboard for monitoring, alarm management, and early intervention

Benefits

We reduce hospital readmission rates by 45% and cardiovascular mortality through continuous monitoring based on scientific evidence, and we reduce associated pathology costs by over 65%.

Hypertension

Our platform for hypertension includes a patient app for recording and monitoring blood pressure with a 7-day protocol and a healthcare dashboard for real-time data, alerts, and comprehensive reports.

Benefits

We increase the percentage of well-controlled patients by 30%, enhancing quality of life and reducing complications.

COPD

Our COPD platform features a patient app for monitoring inspiratory flow, oxygenation, and symptoms, and a healthcare platform for managing the chronic disease cycle.

Benefits

We improve patients' quality of life and reduce readmissions through continuous monitoring.

Atrial Fibrillation

Our AF platform includes a one-channel ECG linked to the patient's mobile app, featuring AI-based algorithms for detecting ectopic beats and AF with high sensitivity and specificity. The healthcare platform allows continuous and personalized follow-up.

Benefits

We increase the percentage of patients receiving comprehensive care, significantly reducing emergency visits and hospitalizations.

Diabetes

Our diabetes platform includes a glucometer linked to the patient's app, with AI-based algorithms detecting trends in blood glucose levels. The healthcare platform provides glucose curves with marked ingestion events and features a computer vision module for detecting food composition.

Benefits

We increase the percentage of patients maintaining glucose levels within limits, resulting in reduced emergency visits and hospitalizations.

Devices

We offer a range of medical devices that automatically connect to our app, transferring multiple health variables directly to the platform. With Internet of Things (IoT) technology, these devices continuously monitor the patient's health and send data in real-time, all with the convenience of being used at home.

OUR PRODUCTS

Platforms and Applications by Pathologies

Heart Failure

 "Our digital platform for monitoring heart failure includes an app for patients that allows them to track their condition daily, as well as a dashboard for the healthcare team that facilitates monitoring, alarm management, and early intervention."

 Benefits: "We reduce hospital readmission rates by 45% and cardiovascular mortality through continuous monitoring based on the best available scientific evidence. Additionally, we reduce associated pathology costs by more than 65%."

Hypertension

 "Our digital platform for hypertension includes an app for patients that allows them to record and monitor their blood pressure through a 7-day protocol with 4 daily pressure readings in accordance with the MDPA (Home Blood Pressure Monitoring) protocol. On the medical side, we provide a dashboard that offers real-time data, alerts for timely interventions, and comprehensive reports that enable correct titration."  Benefits: "We increase the percentage of well-controlled patients by 30%, improving their quality of life and reducing complications."

COPD

 "Our platform for COPD includes a specific app for patients that allows them to monitor their inspiratory flow, oxygenation, and symptoms, and a platform for the healthcare team that facilitates the monitoring and management of chronic disease throughout its cycle."  Benefits: "We improve the quality of life of patients and reduce readmissions through continuous monitoring of the pathology."

Atrial Fibrillation

Our platform for atrial fibrillation (AF) includes a one-channel electrocardiograph (ECG) linked to the patient's mobile app, which contains Artificial Intelligence-based algorithms that detect ectopic beats and the AF condition with high sensitivity and specificity. The platform for the healthcare team allows viewing of tracings and detections for continuous and personalized follow-up."  Benefits: "We increase the percentage of patients receiving comprehensive care, resulting in a significant reduction in emergency visits and hospitalizations."

Diabetes

 "Our platform for Diabetes includes a glucometer linked to the patient's mobile app, which contains Artificial Intelligence-based algorithms that detect with high sensitivity the upward or downward trends in the patient's blood glucose levels. The platform for the healthcare team allows viewing glucose curves with ingestion events marked on them. Additionally, it features a computer vision module capable of detecting the chemical composition of the food the patient is consuming."  Benefits: "We increase the percentage of patients maintaining their glucose levels within limits, resulting in a significant reduction in emergency visits and hospitalizations."

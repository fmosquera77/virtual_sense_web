What is Remote Monitoring?

Remote patient monitoring, within the telemedicine field, involves the use of medical devices and digital platforms to closely monitor patients' health remotely. This technology allows healthcare professionals to detect changes early and take timely actions, without the need for patients to be physically present and with the possibility of doing so asynchronously.

Is There Clinical Evidence of Its Effectiveness?

For several years now, numerous scientific studies have been published showing that remote monitoring improves both the quantity and quality of life for patients with chronic diseases while reducing healthcare system costs.

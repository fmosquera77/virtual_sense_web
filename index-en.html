What is Remote Monitoring?

Remote patient monitoring, within the telemedicine field, involves the use of medical devices and digital platforms to closely monitor patients' health remotely. This technology allows healthcare professionals to detect changes early and take timely actions, without the need for patients to be physically present and with the possibility of doing so asynchronously.

Is There Clinical Evidence of Its Effectiveness?

In recent years, multiple scientific studies have shown that telemonitoring improves both the quantity and quality of life for patients with chronic diseases. This approach not only facilitates more effective symptom management and better adherence to treatments but also reduces healthcare costs by decreasing the frequency of hospitalizations and the need for intensive care, solidifying itself as an essential tool in terms of health.

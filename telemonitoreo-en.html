TELEMONITORING

What is Telemonitoring?

Remote patient monitoring, within the scope of telemedicine, involves the use of medical devices and digital platforms to closely monitor patients' health from a distance. This technology allows healthcare professionals to detect changes early and take timely actions, without the need for patients to be physically present and with the possibility of doing so asynchronously.

Benefits of Telemonitoring

o For the patient: Improves quality of life, makes them feel more cared for and supported.

o For the healthcare team: Provides an innovative service with scientifically proven effectiveness that enables good medical practice while generating new revenue.

o For the system and funders: Significantly reduces costs and provides analytics, metrics, and information that helps make resource use more efficient.
